## Getting There

The closest airport is **Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS)**, about 20
minutes from the venue. Rideshare (Uber/Lyft) and rental cars are both easy
options from the airport.

## Where to Stay

We've reserved a block of rooms at the hotels below. Mention the **"Erin & Ben
Wedding"** when you book to receive our group rate.

| Hotel | Distance | Notes |
| ----- | -------- | ----- |
| The Example Hotel | 5 min to venue | Group rate until Feb 1, 2027 |
| Downtown Suites | 15 min to venue | Great for families |

## Getting Around

We'll provide **shuttle service** between The Example Hotel and the venue on the
wedding day. If you're staying elsewhere, rideshare is widely available
throughout Austin.

## Things to Do

Coming early or staying late? Austin has wonderful food, music, and parks. A few
of our favorites:

- Explore the trails around **Lady Bird Lake**
- Visit the **Texas State Capitol**
- Grab breakfast tacos — ask us for recommendations!
